About Me

My name is Cathy Pedersen. I'm a former QA tester, who is a recent graduate of the Thinkful software engineering bootcamp.

Coding automation was always my favorite part of QA, but I have also enjoyed helping to create clean, bug-free user interfaces. Eventually it occurred to me: what better way to combine these interests and talents than to become a Web developer myself? Learning the ropes of Web development has been more fun than I ever could have imagined.
