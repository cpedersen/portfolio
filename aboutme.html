About Me

My name is Cathy Pedersen. I'm a former Quality Assurance (QA) tester, who is a recent graduate of the Thinkful software engineering bootcamp.

Coding automation was always my favorite part of QA, but I have also enjoyed helping to create clean, bug-free user interfaces. Debugging the next problem in the code or with the system was also a key part of QA that I found both challenging and rewarding. Eventually it occurred to me: what better way to combine these interests and talents than to become a Web developer myself? It's an opportunity to apply what I know about the development and testing of user-friendly interfaces. It's an opportunity to learn new skills. Everyday brings the excitement and challenge of new puzzles to solve.
