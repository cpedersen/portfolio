About Me

Hello, my name is Cathy Pedersen. I'm a Full Stack Developer, who is a recent graduate of the Thinkful software engineering bootcamp, and an experienced test engineer with Python automation development experience.

As a Quality Assurance engineer, I became adept at debugging failed test runs to see whether they had failed because of a bug in the automation code, a change in the features being implemented by Development, a bug in the product, or a lab problem. Coding automation was always my favorite part of Quality Assurance, so I decided to redirect my career toward software development. In this way, I could fully realize my potential as a developer. For me it's an opportunity to apply what I already know about the development and testing of user-friendly interfaces to my own projects. I'm keen to test my own code and to be careful about destabilizing the builds, while I clearly communicate to stakeholders any critical changes. Now everyday brings the excitement of new puzzles to solve as I progress on this development journey.
